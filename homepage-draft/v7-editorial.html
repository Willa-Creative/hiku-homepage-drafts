Our Story

Why hiku exists

Harper started hiku at 15 because she couldn't find skincare that got it. Every clinical brand felt like medicine. Every fun brand burned her face.

So she closed the gap — with real dermatologists, real ingredients, and a belief that your skin doesn't need to be fixed. It needs to be understood.

"It's what she wishes she'd had from the start."
