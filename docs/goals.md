# Hiku — Project Goals

## Drive Conversion
- Position the Discovery Set as the natural starting point
- Reduce overwhelm across products with clear comparison + routine guidance
- Support subscriptions, smart bundling, personalized cart logic

## Build Trust & Emotional Connection
- Lead with Harper's story while balancing derm-backed credibility
- Educate + guide customers about their skin and product choices in a clear, approachable way
- Create participation moments (early access, feedback loops, UGC) to make customers feel included

## Design for Growth
- Build flexible architecture to support future categories (makeup, body, lifestyle)
- Keep routines, bundles, product suggestions flexible so they can be updated and improved over time
