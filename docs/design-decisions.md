### Key Design Principles Established
1. Editorial energy — visually current, not generic DTC
2. Friend with good taste — approachable authority, not brand-talking-at-you
3. Educational without being preachy — guide, don't lecture
4. Credible but culturally fluent — derm-backed but Gen Z native
5. Discovery Set is the hero entry point — design should funnel toward it
