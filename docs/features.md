## Routine Structure (Confirmed)
NOT separate "looks" (glowy vs matte vs poreless as distinct identities). One baseline foundation with flexibility in finish and treatment.

### The Daily Foundation (core ritual)
1. **Cleanse** → Matcha Skin Cleanser
2. **Tone** (optional comfort layer) → Oak Milk Toner
3. **Treat** → Green Juice Serum
4. **Seal — choose your finish** → Guava Glow Moisturizer (glowy) OR Soft Matte Moisturizer (matte)
5. **Lip** → Glacé Kiss Balm

### Supporting Steps (2–3x/week or as needed)
- **Clarify** → Rice Water Clay Mask (congestion)
- **Refine** → Smooth Skin Face Scrub (texture)
- **Target** → Blemish Dots (breakouts)

### Key Framing
- The moisturizer finish is **preference-based** — not a completely different routine
- Supporting steps **layer into** the same foundation, not a separate system
- Through line: **stable + comfortable skin with flexibility between steps**
- This reframes the quiz/routine builder: instead of "what's your skin type?" → "choose your finish, add what you need"

## Key UX Challenge
Uniform packaging + multiple options per routine step = high cognitive load. Site must proactively guide decision-making through structure, storytelling, comparison cues, quizzes, and contextual education so customers feel supported rather than overwhelmed.
