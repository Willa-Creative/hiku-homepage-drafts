# Hiku — Competitor Analysis

Source: Figma "Client Competitors" board (node 24:177) — full homepage screenshots captured

## 1. Summer Fridays (summerfridays.com)
- **Visual approach:** Soft pastels (pink, blue, cream), dreamy gradients, feminine warmth
- **Layout:** Hero product launch banner → community favorites carousel → collection showcase → UGC grid → category cards → editorial modules → moisturizer lineup → brand story → email capture → IG feed
- **Strengths:** Strong balance of commerce + brand storytelling. Product carousels feel curated. Color-blocked sections create visual rhythm. Clean product cards with quick-add.
- **For Hiku:** Good benchmark for shoppable-but-branded homepage. Hiku should feel younger, more editorial, less "California clean girl"

## 2. Glow Recipe (glowrecipe.com)
- **Visual approach:** Bright pink throughout, playful, maximalist, K-beauty-inspired energy
- **Layout:** Hero with product launch → best sellers + new arrivals carousel → "New Routine" UGC → lip & cheek kits → skincare sets → promo (tote GWP) → rewards program (Club Glow) → IG feed
- **Strengths:** Strong rewards integration on homepage. Gift-with-purchase prominent. Category navigation with icons. Heavy product focus.
- **For Hiku:** Too busy/maximalist for Hiku's tone. Rewards program structure is useful reference. Hiku should feel more editorial and less promotional.

## 3. Rhode (rhodeskin.com)
- **Visual approach:** Neutral/earth tones, minimal, cool-girl editorial. Large hero imagery. Muted palette with pops of product color.
- **Layout:** Full-bleed hero → 3-product grid (boost/sculpt/edit) → editorial lifestyle imagery → mask set feature → product carousel → "rhode + you" community section → brand mission (restore/protect/nurture) → accordions (mission, philanthropy, sustainability)
- **Strengths:** Strong editorial energy. Minimal product count makes everything feel intentional. Product naming is simple and action-oriented. Photography is aspirational but approachable.
- **For Hiku:** Closest in tone/vibe to what Hiku could be. But Rhode leans heavily on Hailey Bieber's celebrity — Hiku needs to build brand world through storytelling + community instead. Hiku should be warmer and more playful.

## 4. Sincerely Yours (sincerely-yours.com)
- **Visual approach:** Warm yellows, creams, friendly/approachable energy. Handwritten-style type elements. Lifestyle-forward with UGC.
- **Layout:** Hero with smiling faces → best sellers → "signed off" (derm approval stamp) → starting lineup products → "shop the routine" → lifestyle UGC grid → "love notes" (reviews) → IG feed
- **Strengths:** Strong authenticity angle with derm "signed off" stamp. "Starting lineup" framing is smart for new customers. Lifestyle UGC feels genuine. Warm, friendly tone throughout.
- **For Hiku:** Similar audience target + derm-credibility angle. Sincerely Yours is warmer/softer — Hiku should feel cooler, more British, more editorial while maintaining warmth. The "starting lineup" concept is similar to Hiku's Discovery Set strategy.

## 5. Laneige (us.laneige.com)
- **Visual approach:** Pink/blue color blocking, K-beauty polish, product-first. Clean and commercial.
- **Layout:** Hero product launch → product grid with tabs (lip products, best sellers, new) → "turn up the tint" education → video/editorial → limited edition → UGC (#fallsinlovewithlaneige) → personalized recs → email capture
- **Strengths:** Strong product education modules. Tab-based product browsing. Limited edition drops create urgency. Clean product photography.
- **For Hiku:** Too commercial/corporate for Hiku's tone. Product education approach (explaining what products do in simple terms) is relevant. Hiku needs more brand personality and storytelling.

## Competitive Positioning Summary

| Brand | Tone | Visual Energy | Hiku Differentiator |
|---|---|---|---|
| Summer Fridays | Warm, Californian, soft | Pastel, dreamy | Hiku is cooler, more British, more editorial |
| Glow Recipe | Playful, maximalist, K-beauty | Bright pink, busy | Hiku is more intentional, less noisy |
| Rhode | Cool-girl, minimal, aspirational | Neutral, editorial | Hiku is warmer, community-driven (no celeb) |
| Sincerely Yours | Friendly, authentic, warm | Yellow, lifestyle-forward | Hiku is cooler, more culturally fluent |
| Laneige | Commercial, polished, K-beauty | Pink/blue, product-first | Hiku is more editorial, less corporate |

## Where Hiku Sits
- More editorial than all competitors (NYLON energy)
- More British-inflected than any competitor (transatlantic cool)
- Younger founder story (Harper, 15) vs. celebrity/corporate
- Warmer than Rhode, cooler than Sincerely Yours
- More intentional than Glow Recipe, more personality than Laneige
- Community participation ("made with you") vs. traditional influencer marketing
