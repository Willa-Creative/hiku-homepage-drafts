## The Challenge
How might we create a skincare experience that respects developing skin while being genuinely engaging to explore — bridging the gap between clinical efficacy and cultural relevance for Gen Z?

## The Opportunity

| Pillar | Description |
|---|---|
| **Unmet Need** | Gen Z wants skincare that supports who they already are, not who they're told to be. A trusted friend, not another brand talking at them. |
| **Market Position** | Bridge the gap between clinical efficacy and cultural relevance. British sensibility meets K-beauty playful exploration. |
| **Digital Experience** | Create a space that educates without preaching, delights without overwhelming, and earns trust through thoughtful design. |

## Experience Vision: "The British Dollhouse of Curiosities"

> A digital townhouse where each room reveals gentle discoveries, blending British propriety with playful exploration.

**Formula:** Gentle product philosophy x Playful exploratory experience = A space that respects your skin while inviting curiosity.

### The Dollhouse Metaphor

| Principle | Description |
|---|---|
| **Orderly on the inside** | Clean, organized British sensibility. The navigation is intuitive, the layout is refined, nothing feels chaotic. |
| **Delightfully curious within** | Each room invites exploration. Unexpected details, playful interactions, moments of discovery. |
| **Rooms, not pages** | The site isn't a linear flow — it's a space to wander. Education, product, community — each has its place. |

## Experience Principles (6 Total)

### 1. Gentle First, Playful Second
The experience always feels soothing and breathable before it feels delightful. Playful interactions act as small rewards, never noise.

### 2. Exploration Over Navigation
The site is designed like a townhouse you explore room-by-room, not a catalog you scroll. Discovery feels natural, calm, and curiosity-led.

### 3. Ritual Over Routine
Navigation and journeys revolve around building a simple daily ritual, not comparing endless products. Every path leads toward clarity and confidence.

### 4. British on the Outside, Curious Within
Polished and composed at first glance, the experience reveals delightful hidden details as you explore. Information unfolds through drawers, tiles, and compartments like a bento box of curiosities — making learning feel light, playful, and easy to digest.

### 5. Trusted Friend Energy
The site behaves like a warm beauty concierge — reassuring, knowledgeable, and never pushy. Every interaction builds confidence and removes pressure. Works for teens while earning parent trust through transparency.

### 6. Curated Like a Department Store
Content and products feel hand-picked and editorial, like wandering a luxury beauty floor. Fewer, better choices reduce overwhelm and build trust.

## Experience Tactics (Interaction Ideas)

### Product Discovery
- **'Try it on'** — Interactive texture/color previews. See how the cleanser lathers, watch the moisturizer absorb. Small delights that show you how it works.
- **Product Hovers** — Oozing texture videos revealed on hover of products
- **Product Page Interaction** — Scroll through Ritual and watch products 'layer' onto skin, showing how they work together

### Easter Eggs & Delight
- **Easter Egg** — Find the secret door to reveal an illustrated townhouse version of the website — interactions bring the concept to life in a fun way
- **Sticker Takeover** — Once you scroll to the end of the site, hiku stickers quickly begin to overlap and take over
- **Build Your Routine** — Fun paper doll-like cut-out interactions to customize your routine into fun art and share it

### Cart & Post-Purchase
- **Cart Experience** — Products arrange themselves like items on a vanity table when added to cart
- **Thank You Page** — After purchase, a hand-written note from Harper appears. Feels personal, not automated.

### Education & Personalization
- **British Translation*** — Explain product ingredients and benefits with fun AI translator or in Harper's voice
- **Skin Wardrobing*** — Comparing formulation, texture and thickness to your favorite wardrobe pieces
- **Beauty Quiz*** — Interactive skincare quiz set inside the Hiku House, where each feature unlocks a step of your personalized AM + PM routine. Users build a regimen that feels tailored, intuitive, and ready to shop.

(*asterisk = stretch/concept ideas*)

### Mobile Interactions
- Interactive dice & emoji response
- Horizontal scroll & skin quiz
- Interactive glider ("how's your skin today?")
- Crystal ball oracle (could be pink pearlescent bubbles)
